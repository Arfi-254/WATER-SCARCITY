The Crisis

Regional Water Challenges

🌵

Severe Drought

Prolonged droughts often dry up seasonal rivers, leaving communities without stable water for months.

⚙️

Broken Infrastructure

Boreholes and pumps often fall into disrepair, and without quick reporting, they remain broken for weeks.

📍

Remote Locations

Many pastoralist communities travel over 20km to reach the nearest functional water point.

💧

Water Quality

Contamination in open water pans causes preventable diseases that impact the most vulnerable.

Action

Water Conservation Tips

Harvest Rainwater

Capture runoff from roofs during the rainy season to use for livestock or cleaning.

Fix Leaks Fast

Even small leaks in pipes or taps can waste dozens of liters every day.

Reuse Greywater

Use water from washing clothes or vegetables to water gardens or suppress dust.
